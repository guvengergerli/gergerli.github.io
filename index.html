About Me

My name is Güven Gergerli, and I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Purdue University, working in the CAMP Lab under the supervision of Prof. Joseph Campbell. My research focuses on Theory of Mind, reinforcement learning, uncertainty quantification, and multi-agent systems. Currently, I am investigating uncertainty-based Bayesian reasoning in multi-agent environments. I earned my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Bilkent University in 2023.
